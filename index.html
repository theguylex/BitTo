Let's start here

We have prepared a list of frequently asked questions to onboard our newbies.

Cryptocurrency (or “crypto”) is a digital currency that can be used to buy goods and services or traded for a profit. Bitcoin is the most widely used cryptocurrency.

Blockchain is the technology that enables the existence of cryptocurrency (among other things). Bitcoin is the name of the most recognized cryptocurrency, the one for which blockchain technology, as we currently know it, was created.

Blockchain is a shared, immutable ledger that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a business network.

Crypto wallets store your private keys and keep your crypto safe and accessible for spending, sending, or saving.

Crypto mining is a process blockchain networks, like Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, use to finalize transactions. It's called mining because this process also releases new coins into circulation.
